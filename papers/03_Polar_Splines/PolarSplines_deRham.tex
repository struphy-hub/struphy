\begin{abstract}
We construct the de Rham sequence of conforming tensor product B-spline spaces for mappings with a polar singularity. The sequence is exact ($\grad V_0 = \ker V_1$, $\curl V_1 = \ker V_2$) and can be fit into the commuting diagram of finite element exterior calculus (FEEC). In the mapped domain, basis functions are $C^2$ at the pole in the first space $V_0$ and are $C^0$ at the pole in the third space $V_3$. 
\end{abstract}
